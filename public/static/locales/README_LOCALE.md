**Translation content**

By default, `next-i18next` expects your translations to be organised as such:
```
.
└── public
    └── static
        └── locales
            ├── en
            |   └── home.json
            |   └── header.json
            |   └── footer.json
            └── de
                └── home.json
                └── header.json
                └── footer.json
```

This structure can also be seen in the [simple example](https://github.com/isaachinman/next-i18next/tree/master/examples/simple).
